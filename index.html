Sea Otter

Otter grooming includes cheek rubbing and teeth cleaning

  • Scientific Name: Enhydra lutris
  • Average Lenght: 125 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: North Pacific coast

Sea Otters are exellent swimmers and spend their days catching fish and teaching their pups handy tricks, like how to use stones to break open shellfish.

Canadian Sphynx

Sphynxes are known for their extroverted behavior.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Lenght: 6-12 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 8-14 years
  • Habitat: The human heart

The largely hairless Sphynx is very people-oriented and affectionate. They are highly intelligent, playful, cuddly, often described as being "part dog, part child, part monkey, and part cat". They display a high level of energy, intelligence, curiosity, and affection for their owners
